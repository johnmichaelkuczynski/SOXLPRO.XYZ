**BUILD SOXL-QQQ RELATIVE DISLOCATION SURFACE**

A new app (separate from the IV surface tool) that identifies when SOXL shares are over- or under-responding to QQQ relative to their structural leverage relationship, and estimates mean-reversion timing.

**1. DATA INGESTION**
- Pull 3 years of daily OHLCV for SOXL and QQQ (use yfinance or Polygon).
- Align on trading days. Drop any day with missing data on either.
- Compute daily log returns for both.

**2. ROLLING BETA CALCULATION**
- Compute rolling beta of SOXL to QQQ over windows: 5d, 10d, 20d, 60d, 120d.
- Beta = Cov(SOXL_ret, QQQ_ret) / Var(QQQ_ret) over the window.
- Store all five beta series. Expected range: ~2.8 to ~3.5 (structural target is ~3.3).

**3. RESIDUAL COMPUTATION**
For each day and each lookback window:
- `expected_SOXL_return = beta_window × QQQ_return`
- `residual = actual_SOXL_return - expected_SOXL_return`
- `cumulative_residual_Nd = sum of residuals over trailing N days` for N in [1, 5, 10, 20]

**4. Z-SCORE NORMALIZATION**
- For each (lookback, cumulative_window) pair, compute z-score of current cumulative residual vs. its own 1-year distribution.
- Output: matrix of z-scores across (beta_lookback × residual_window).

**5. MEAN-REVERSION ANALYSIS**
Historical study (compute once, cache):
- For every historical day where |cumulative_residual_20d z-score| > 1.5, measure:
  - Days until residual returns to within ±0.5 z-score
  - Magnitude of reversion move
- Bin by starting z-score magnitude. Output lookup table: "z-score X → median Y days to revert, median Z% move."

**6. SURFACE VISUALIZATION (Plotly 3D)**
- X-axis: beta lookback window (5, 10, 20, 60, 120 days)
- Y-axis: residual accumulation window (1, 5, 10, 20 days)
- Z-axis: current z-score of residual
- Color: red for positive (SOXL overshot, expect pullback), blue for negative (SOXL undershot, expect catch-up)
- Overlay a flat reference plane at z = 0.

**7. RECOMMENDATION PANEL**
- If any cell shows |z| > 2.0: flag as "EXTREME DISLOCATION"
- Show: current z-score, historical median days-to-revert for that magnitude, historical hit rate (% of times reversion occurred within 2× median), suggested direction (long SOXL if z < -2, short SOXL / long SOXS if z > +2).
- Show realized vs. expected SOXL price: "SOXL trading at $98.25; structural model implies $X based on QQQ path."

**8. DIAGNOSTIC PANEL**
- Current SOXL beta to QQQ (20d rolling)
- Current QQQ 20d realized vol
- Current SOXL 20d realized vol
- Ratio of actual SOXL vol to (3.3 × QQQ vol) — this quantifies volatility decay drag
- Last 20 days: table of daily residuals

**9. BACKTEST MODULE**
- Simulate strategy: enter when |z| > 2.0 (20d residual, 20d beta), exit when z crosses 0 or 10 days elapse.
- Report: win rate, avg return per trade, Sharpe, max drawdown over 3-year period.
- Without a passing backtest, do not trust the signal.
